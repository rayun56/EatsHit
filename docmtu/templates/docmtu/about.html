What?

EatsHit is a webapp made to give easier access to menus for Michigan Tech's dining halls.

Why?

DineOnCampus's website is slow, unresponsive, confusingly laid out, and borderline broken at times.

How?

As bad as their website is, DineOnCampus actually has a very navigable API. Looking at the HTTP requests while navigating the official site was enough for me to reverse engineer their backend API to make my own requests.

Where?

Uh, here. EatsHit.tech.

Who?

As you might have guessed, I'm a student at MTU. You can contact me at goand@EatsHit.tech, or check out my personal website rayun.dev

When?

I would like to keep this site running as long as I have the ability to keep hosting it, and presumably as long as MTU sticks with DineOnCampus.
